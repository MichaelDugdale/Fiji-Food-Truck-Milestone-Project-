A little bit about us!

Our philosophy

Growing up on fiji has always been a blessing and like most fijians im ever grateful and looking for a way to give back and protect our home. By Ethically sourcing our products and being environmentally conscious we can do just that by reducing our carbon footprint and protecting future generation from climate change. Save the world, enjoy our tacos!

Our Food

The freshest locally sourced, organically grown ingredients free from any GMO. Our menu's can change daily depending on the chef's inspiration & market availability of seasonal produce! Our staple menu consisits burgers, burritos, tacos, bao buns, subs and poki bowls!

Our Family

Proud to say the Fiji food truck is a Family run business, started by and run by three brothers. Jarrel, Randel and Curtis Kamea, a decdicated and highly experienced team who are here to provide amazing food matched by amazing hospitality Fiji has come to be known for. Incredible food while reducing our carbon footprint? whats not to love!
