A little bit about us!

Our philosophy

We are the Kamea boys and we all grew up in Fiji. We have all spent time overseas working and travelling but when it came to time to settle down, we decided to build a business here at home in Fiji. We want to give back to the country of our birth and we do that by sourcing fresh local produce on a daily basis and keeping our carbon footprint as low as possible.

Our Food

I am sure most of you have seen Anil as he does our marketing every day. He sources the freshest locally sourced, organically grown ingredients free from any GMO. Our menu's can change daily depending on the chef's inspiration & market availability of seasonal produce! Our staple menu consists burgers, burritos, tacos, bao buns, subs and poke bowls!

Our Family

Proud to say the Fiji food truck is a Family run business, started by me, Jarrel and my brothers Randall and Curtis. a dedicated and highly experienced team who are here to provide amazing food matched by the amazing hospitality Fiji has come to be known for.
